What did we do well?

  • Lots of pop-ups
    • Some work isn't reflected in the sprint
  • Automated testing might need IT support and a project which could take a long time.
  • Many tasks were blocked due to late DUT arrivals
    • 8 module hasn't arrived
    • Beta 2 pack might have fried VRFM
  • Didn't take sprint deadline seriously
  • Sprint planning meeting needs to take the time to break tasks into bite-sized pieces (if possible)

What should we have done better?

  • Pull pop-ups into the sprint and bump other things out. Paul Rabahy, Justin Hermannreally likes the concept of the sprint plan. If the team keeps trying this, eventually other teams will accept that they just need to plan ahead a few weeks to get their work into the sprint. It would take a few months to establish a new norm. I encourage the team to stay the course. If there's no benefit after 6  months, then so be it. 
  • Don't work on tasks that don't have Jira tickets associated. Paul Rabahy, Justin Hermann fully supports. I know Eric will as well. I realize this is difficult for people to "require a ticket", but in a situation with more work than people, we probably have to push for this.
