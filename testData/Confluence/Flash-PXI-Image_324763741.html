BTSI Lab Systems Integration : Flash PXI Image

Created by Collin Winans on Feb 18, 2025

The PXI can be easily flashed with a backup image. This is useful for quickly reinstalling the base software after a repair, etc.

The Replication and Deployment (RAD) utility can be downloaded from NI here: https://www.ni.com/en/support/downloads/software-products/download.replication-and-deployment-utility.html#333535
