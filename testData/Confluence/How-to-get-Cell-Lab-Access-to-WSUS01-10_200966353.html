BTSI Lab Systems Integration : How to get Cell Lab Access to WSUS01-10

Created by Chelsea Flattery, last updated on Jun 08, 2023

Cell Lab users utilize Remote Desktop Connection to access the workstations for monitoring.  To get them access is a two step process.

Step-by-step guide

The Galileo Part

  1. Create a Galileo ticket for Active Directory Access 

  2. Mimic the information below into the ticket

    ItemSelected
    Project149700 - Bucket-GPDQ L2/3 Escalations, App Due Diligence, Security Maintenance 2022
    ASMS15309 - iCentral
    Action TypeAD Group Management
    ActionAdd Members to Group
    AD Group DomainNAM
    AD GroupNAM_VEHTEST_L_CELLRD_USERS
    Group OwnerPZQJXW - Chelsea Flattery
    Account TypeNormal
    Account DomainNAM
  3. Add the user(s) who need access to the group

The IT Store Part:

  1. Fill out a TCWS Technician Assistance ticket
  2. Each user added to the group above now needs their laptop hostname added to the Functional ID of the computer(s) they need to access
  3. Include the hostname and functional ID of each computer the user(s) need access to and list the user's laptop hostname in the ticket
    1. Check the inventory sheet for the most up to date PC hostnames and FIDs


This process may take up to 48-hours to fully process
