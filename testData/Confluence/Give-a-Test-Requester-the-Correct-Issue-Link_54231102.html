BTSI Lab Systems Integration : Give a Test Requester the Correct Issue Link

Created by Chelsea Flattery on Jun 01, 2020

If a test, validation, or dev engineer asks for the link to a Jira Service Desk ticket, there are two methods to use to provide them with information.  

Step-by-step guide

If they are the original issue reporter:

  1. Find their issue from in the Jira Service Desk queue
  2. Within the issue page, select the View customer request
  3. Provide the URL in the address bar

If they are not the issue reporter:

  1. Follow steps 1 and 2
  2. In the service desk portal page, click the Share button
  3. Enter the name of the user or users who want to be able to view and comment on the issue then click Share
  4. Provide the URL in the address bar
  5. Users can also access all of the issues they have created or been listed as a participant on by clicking their Requests button
  6. They will see a filter for My requests and All requests, for the issues they participate in
  7. Changing the second filter on this page to Created by anyone will expand the selection to all request that have been shared with them or that they have created


Issue creators can also share their issue
