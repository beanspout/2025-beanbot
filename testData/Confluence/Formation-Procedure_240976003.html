BTSI Lab Systems Integration : Formation Procedure

Created by Collin Winans on Jan 05, 2024

Formation 1

  1. Rest 1 hour (adjustable based on test scheduling)
  2. Charge (C/10) for 30 seconds
  3. Voltage check, confirm voltage is less than 3.6V
  4. Rest 10 minutes
  5. 1st Formation Charge: (C/10), 3.95V CC-CV, cutoff at 5A or (C/100) or after 3 hours of CV (whichever comes first)
  6. Rest 2 hours
  7. Set chamber to 45째C, wait for cell to reach 40째C
  8. Rest 24 hours
  9. Set Chamber to 23째C, wait for cell to reach 28째C
  10. Rest 2 hours


(At this point the cells are removed from the test lab and sent to de-gas)


Formation 2

  1. Rest 1 hour
  2. Voltage check, confirm voltage is greater than 3.5V
  3. Rest 10 minutes
  4. Charge: (C/10), 4.2V CC-CV, cutoff at 5A or (C/100) or after 3 hours of CV (whichever comes first)
  5. Rest 30 minutes
  6. Discharge: (C/5), stop at 3.736V (no CV)
  7. Rest 30 minutes
  8. DCIR
    1. (1C) discharge, 20 seconds
    2. Rest 20 seconds
    3. (1C) charge, 20 seconds
    4. Rest 60 seconds
  9. Discharge: (C/5), stop at 2.5V (no CV)
  10. Rest 30 minutes
  11. Charge: (C/5), stop at 3.6V (no CV)
  12. Rest 1 hour
