Goals

  • How will the 2 teams handle controlling and communicating with the packs?
    • Cell voltages and temperatures
    • Opening/Closing contactors
    • Recording other data provided by the pack
  • Which team is responsible for writing the test scripts that will be run by S&A?
    • If not, what will we use instead?
    • If so, are there any that need to be added to Solution_Common?
  • Can the LSIE team use Solution_Common to support S&A?
  • Does the LSIE team need to support any additional testing equipment?
    • Should the LSIE team support data rates of over 100hz?
    • Should the LSIE team support Dewesoft?
    • Are there any additional devices (crush machine, short circuit fixture, etc.) that we should add to Solution_Common?

Non-Goals

  • Calibration of equipment is out of scope for this meeting.
    • I will schedule another meeting to figure out a more effective way of supporting calibration.
