AD Groups:
To change ownership of AD Groups the current (as of Oct 2024) method is to submit a Galileo request. 

Galileo >Services > Access Management > IAM-AD Active Directory Security Group Request

NAM_VEHTEST_G_VEHHYB_USERS - Full control, for team members who need access to the Z:, S:, and T: drives

NAM_VEHTEST_L_VEHHYB_USERS - Read & Execute, for team members who need access to the Z:, S:, and T: drives

NAM_ETNET_G_VEHHYB_OWNER - 

NAM_ETNET_G_VEHHYB -

NAM_VEHTEST_G_VEHHYB_FIDS - Full control, for Functional IDs only

NAM_VEHTEST_L_VEHHYB_FIDS - Read & Execute, for Functional IDs only (largely unused)

NAM_VEHTEST_G_VEHHYB_CONFIG_READONLY - Read & Execute
