BTSI Lab Systems Integration : How to add a user to the LSIE Jenkins-k8s instance

Created by Constantin Balan on Nov 15, 2022

This article is meant for Jenkins administrators. If you aren't listed as an administrator in Jenkins, you won't be able to access this flow.

Step 1:

First you'll have to navigate to the Jenkins-k8s link: https://jenkins-52214.op7mi.gm.com/
If you're not already logged in, your GMID & password used for logging into your laptop is used here as well.

Step 2:

The next step once you're logged in is to click the "Manage Jenkins" tab on the left hand side of the page (pictured below).

Step 3:

Next, scroll down on the page until you see the "Configure Global Security" tab under the

"Security" section and click on it.

Step 4:

Below is the Configure Global Security page. If you scroll down to about the middle of the page, you will

see the user security matrix. For a majority of the users you are adding, we will be assigning them the "Read"

role under the "Overall" columns. Eventually we can specify more specific permissions, but other LSIE members

should be able to see what they need with that generic assigned role for now (More instructions below).

Once here, you click on the "Add user or group..." button, and the following prompt should show up:

Here you enter in the GMID of the user you are trying to register. Once you do, the user will be added at the

bottom of the matrix (At this point their first and last name should take the place of their GMID). You can then check 

the "Read" checkbox (mentioned above), and then click save at the bottom of the screen.


Congrats! You've added a user to the Jenkins-k8s instance
