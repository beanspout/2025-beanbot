There are two cycler models: PSB 10010-1000 and PSB 10010-340


Here is a backdoor process to flash firmware on the KE unit (communication unit) of the cyclers. If the update fails and bricks the cycler, this should also be able to recover it. 

I don't currently have a backdoor process to update the HMI (screen) or DR (power stage)
