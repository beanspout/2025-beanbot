BTSI Lab Systems Integration : Markdown

Created by Chelsea Flattery on Jan 27, 2020

JIRA systems like Jira, Service Desk, and BitBucket use Markdown, but they're not as willing to help with formatting like Confluence and GitLab.  Here's a link to a helpful cheat sheet and some quick tips for when you want to make your BitBucket comments and descriptions have the same nice formatting as GitLab.
