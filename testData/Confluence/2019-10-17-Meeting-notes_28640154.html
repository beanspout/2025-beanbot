safety/intro
  • TC24 has some safety&abuse testing going on in it right now. Main takeaway is that it's been vetted pretty thoroughly and the additional bracing and stuff is just precaution. They have several mitigation plans/layers in place, informed by lots of other tests and data. Everything should be fine. The ratchet straps are there mostly as a visual indicator to not mess with the chamber, not to "hold the chamber together just in case". We're gonna get chamber 61 for the next couple weeks so we don't have to be near TC24. It's fine to work near there, the risk is considered "mitigated". Construction teams are still able to work in the area as well. If you feel unsafe, say something, there's no pressure to be around (or in) a situation where you feel unsafe.

calibrationsmike
  • there's a "quality box" that the cell team has that mike would like to integrate
  • want to get rid of "pre-calibration" activities so we don't have to get involved with the cal process ourselves (or cut out other orgs or whatever). Currently we do pre-cal, which is basically a calibration on its own, in preparation for IKC to come do their "actual" calibration

  • nick spends the majority of his time doing "pre-calibration" work as well
