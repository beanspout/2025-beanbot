Overview¶

The configuration options impact all iTest solutions on the PC. Many of these options define the communications between iTest and LabCentral, LabMinder, LabAgent, or any of the other LabWorX products.

iTest INI Options¶

The following options are specific to iTest and are not exclusive to interaction with the LabWorX products.
