iTest Advanced INI Options¶

These powertek.ini file options were either designed to overcome very unique customer requirements in the field or for debugging and overcoming unique situations. Unless you know that these options will solve a specific problem you are having, it is best not to use them. Many are retained for backwards compatibility with our existing iTest applications.
